Artifical Intelligence Course: CS50-AI

After finishing RoboCup in the summer of 2023 I decided that I wanted something to continue my programming skills and not let them stagniate. The previous year my programming teacher introduced me to CS50, a series of computer science courses offered by Harvard which upon completion would offer a certificate along with valuable knowledge to take with you into the future.

The course was valuable, and I learned many fascinating about the development of artificial intelligence. I credit it with giving my programming ability a massive boost. I was introduced to many subjects, including set theory, probability/statistics, machine learning, among many others. If you are interested, you can find the GitHub repository here.
